# ND30 LLM review prompt contract

Use the **default model currently running inside QwenPaw**.
Do not call an external LLM service.

## Review scope
- spelling and punctuation
- administrative wording and tone
- logic across title / subject / legal bases / body / recipients / sign-off
- consistency of agencies, dates, symbols, names, abbreviations
- normalization of repetitive phrasing

## Safety
- do not invent facts
- do not silently change legal bases, signatory, authority, date, number, symbol, recipient scope, or mandatory assignments
- keep the content report separate from the layout report by default
