# ND30 Document Template Guide

## 1. Công văn
Use for exchange, request, guidance, coordination, or reply.

### Required core parts
- agency block
- number and symbol
- location and date
- `V/v ...`
- `Kính gửi`
- body paragraphs
- signature block
- `Nơi nhận`
- optional contact line at the bottom of page 1

## 2. Văn bản có tên loại
Use for forms like `THÔNG BÁO`, `KẾ HOẠCH`, `BÁO CÁO`, `TỜ TRÌNH`, `HƯỚNG DẪN`, `PHƯƠNG ÁN`.

### Required core parts
- agency block
- number and symbol
- location and date
- uppercase title
- bold trích yếu
- body text
- signature block
- `Nơi nhận`

## 3. Quyết định trực tiếp
Use when the decision itself is written into the body.

### Required core parts
- title `QUYẾT ĐỊNH`
- `Về việc ...`
- authority line
- legal bases
- `QUYẾT ĐỊNH:`
- articles (`Điều 1`, `Điều 2`, ...)
- signature block
- `Nơi nhận`

## 4. Quyết định gián tiếp
Use when the decision promulgates or approves another attached text.

### Distinguishing sign
The body often starts with `Điều 1. Ban hành/Phê duyệt kèm theo Quyết định này ...`

## 5. Giấy mời
### Key content
- person or organization invited
- event name
- chair
- time
- location
- notes for preparation or attendance

## 6. Giấy giới thiệu
### Key content
- introduced person
- position/unit
- destination organization
- purpose of work
- validity date if needed

## 7. Biên bản
### Key content
- meeting or event name
- start time
- location
- attendees
- chair
- secretary
- meeting minutes summary
- end time
- signature blocks for secretary and chair

## 8. Giấy nghỉ phép
### Key content
- applicant name and position
- leave duration
- leave dates
- place of leave
- leave category basis
- signer block
- optional confirmation block

## Practical drafting rule
When a user brings messy notes, first normalize them into one of the above forms. Do not preserve chat-like wording. Convert it into formal administrative phrasing appropriate to the chosen form.
