# Capitalization and Abbreviations under ND30

## Capitalization reminders
- Capitalize the first letter of each component in Vietnamese personal names.
- Capitalize official geographic names and agency names correctly.
- Capitalize document titles when naming a specific legal or administrative document.
- Capitalize titles such as `Chủ tịch`, `Thủ tướng`, `Giám đốc` when attached to a specific named person in the running text where appropriate.

## Copy forms
- Sao y: `SY`
- Trích sao: `TrS`
- Sao lục: `SL`

## Practical symbol rule
For named documents, the symbol usually combines document-type abbreviation and issuing-unit abbreviation.
For công văn, the symbol usually combines issuing-unit abbreviation and drafting unit or field abbreviation.
