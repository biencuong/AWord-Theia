# ND30 source map

This file maps the legal sources bundled by the user into operational responsibilities for this skill.

## Core legal basis

- **Nghị định 30/2020/NĐ-CP**: defines the scope of administrative document work, the list of document types, the requirement to draft documents đúng hình thức, thể thức, và kỹ thuật trình bày, and the authority/signature workflow.
- **Phụ lục I**: mandatory rules for page setup, layout positions, font, size ladder, signature block, nơi nhận, mật/khẩn marks, phụ lục, and bản sao.
- **Phụ lục II**: capitalization rules in administrative writing.
- **Phụ lục III**: abbreviations for document symbols and sample layouts by document type.
- **Phụ lục IV**: management forms such as registration books and incoming-document handling forms.
- **Phụ lục V**: hồ sơ and nộp lưu forms.
- **Phụ lục VI**: electronic records management requirements.

## What this skill covers directly

This skill focuses on:

1. Choosing the right **document family**.
2. Drafting or normalizing text into **administrative style**.
3. Generating `.docx` files with **ND30-oriented defaults**.
4. Checking **formatting and logical completeness** before delivery.

## What is only lightly covered here

- Registry books and archival management forms from Phụ lục IV and V.
- Full electronic records-system behavior from Phụ lục VI.

These annexes are still relevant as references, but this skill is primarily about producing or reviewing official administrative `.docx` files.


## Practical scope extension

In this skill, Appendix IV and Appendix V primarily inform the workbook/register patterns and filing-support sheets. Appendix I-III remain the primary source for Word-document drafting and review.
