# ND30 writing and abbreviations

## Capitalization

Use the ND30 capitalization rules for:

- personal names
- Vietnamese and foreign place names
- agency and organization names
- titles of laws, decrees, resolutions, reports, and official commemorative names
- named historical events and administrative units

### Practical reminders

- Capitalize all syllables in Vietnamese personal names.
- Capitalize the specific proper-name part of administrative place names.
- Capitalize agency names according to their official structure.
- Capitalize the document type when naming a specific legal/administrative document, for example `Nghị định số 30/2020/NĐ-CP`.

## Practical drafting notes

- Keep tone formal and administrative.
- Avoid spoken/chat style language.
- First citation of a legal basis should be complete enough to identify the instrument.
- Later citations may be shortened if the reference is already clear.
