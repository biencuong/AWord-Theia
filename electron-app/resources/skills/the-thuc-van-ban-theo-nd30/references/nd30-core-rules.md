# ND30 core rules

## Page setup

- Paper size: **A4**.
- Orientation: normally **portrait**; landscape only when truly needed for wide tables.
- Margins:
  - top: **20–25 mm**
  - bottom: **20–25 mm**
  - left: **30–35 mm**
  - right: **15–20 mm**
- Font: **Times New Roman**, Unicode, black.
- Body text size: usually **13–14 pt**.

## National title block

On the first page, the national title block is on the **upper right**:

- `CỘNG HÒA XÃ HỘI CHỦ NGHĨA VIỆT NAM` — uppercase, bold, usually 12–13 pt
- `Độc lập - Tự do - Hạnh phúc` — bold, usually 13–14 pt, centered under the national title
- a horizontal line under the tiêu ngữ

## Issuing agency block

On the upper left:

- direct parent agency if any, uppercase, usually 12–13 pt
- issuing agency, uppercase and bold, usually 12–13 pt
- a horizontal line under the issuing agency

## Number and symbol

- Format with `Số:` followed by number and symbol.
- Number uses Arabic numerals.
- When the number is below 10, prefer a leading zero if the organization's practice requires it.
- Symbol uses official abbreviations and hyphen grouping.

## Location and date line

- Same horizontal band as number/symbol, on the right side.
- Italic style.
- Format like: `Hà Giang, ngày 15 tháng 4 năm 2026`.
- Day and month under 10 should be zero-padded when following the strict rule.

## Title and summary block

### Documents with a named type

- Title line: uppercase, bold.
- Summary/trích yếu: bold, centered under the title.
- Horizontal line under the summary.

### Công văn

- No separate big title block.
- Use subject line after `V/v` under the number-symbol area.
- Add `Kính gửi` block before the body.

## Body text

- Use regular sentence case, justified.
- First-line indent: **1 cm** or **1.27 cm**.
- Paragraph spacing after: at least **6 pt** is a good default.
- Line spacing: between single and 1.5 lines.
- For decisions and structured legal text, use `Điều`, `khoản`, `điểm` correctly.

## Signer block

- Right side near the end of the document.
- Authority prefixes may include `TM.`, `KT.`, `TL.`, `TUQ.`, or `Q.` when legally appropriate.
- Office line is uppercase, bold.
- Signer name is bold, centered under the signature space.
- Do not prepend academic titles unless sector rules explicitly allow it.

## Nơi nhận

### Công văn, báo cáo, tờ trình gửi cấp trên

Use two parts:

1. `Kính gửi:` for the direct recipient(s)
2. `Nơi nhận:` for related recipients and archival line

### Other document types

Usually only `Nơi nhận:` is required.

Formatting pattern:

- `Nơi nhận:` italic + bold, usually 12 pt
- recipient lines usually 11 pt, each on its own line beginning with `-`
- final archival line normally includes `Lưu: VT, ...`

## Additional marks

When applicable, include:

- độ mật mark
- mức độ khẩn mark
- phạm vi lưu hành box
- drafter mark and issued-copy count
- contact line/footer metadata

Use these only when the situation requires them.
