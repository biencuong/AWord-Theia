# ND30 document-type matrix

Use this file to choose the correct document family before drafting.

## Matrix

### Công văn

Use when the main purpose is to request, reply, guide, urge, coordinate, report by letter, or transmit instructions to one or more recipients.

Signals:

- has direct addressee(s)
- often starts with `Kính gửi:`
- subject line usually starts with `V/v ...`

### Nghị quyết (cá biệt)

Use when a collective authority issues a resolution for a specific matter.

Signals:

- the issuing authority is a council or collective body
- body often ends in `QUYẾT NGHỊ:`

### Quyết định (trực tiếp)

Use when the decision text itself contains operative rules or instructions, usually split into articles.

Signals:

- title starts with `QUYẾT ĐỊNH`
- body contains `Điều 1`, `Điều 2`, ...

### Quyết định (gián tiếp)

Use when the decision mainly promulgates, approves, or attaches another document.

Signals:

- title often starts with `Ban hành ...` or `Phê duyệt ...`
- Article 1 often says the attached document is promulgated/approved together with the decision

### Văn bản có tên loại

Use for named forms that share the standard title + summary layout, for example:

- thông báo
- hướng dẫn
- chương trình
- kế hoạch
- phương án
- đề án
- dự án
- báo cáo
- tờ trình
- hợp đồng
- bản ghi nhớ
- bản thỏa thuận
- giấy ủy quyền
- phiếu gửi
- phiếu chuyển
- phiếu báo
- thư công

### Công điện

Use for urgent telegraphic/dispatch-style communication.

Signals:

- very urgent or operational content
- dedicated title `CÔNG ĐIỆN`

### Giấy mời

Use to invite a person or organization to a meeting, conference, ceremony, or working session.

### Giấy giới thiệu

Use to formally introduce a person sent to work with another agency or organization.

### Biên bản

Use to record a meeting, event, working session, inspection, or other proceeding.

### Giấy nghỉ phép

Use to grant leave according to a leave request.

### Phụ lục

Use for content attached to a main document and explicitly referenced by that document.

### Bản sao giấy / điện tử

Use when preparing `SAO Y`, `SAO LỤC`, or `TRÍCH SAO` according to ND30 copy rules.

## Escalation rule when unsure

If the user's request could fit either `công văn` or a named form:

- choose `công văn` when there is a direct recipient and a communication purpose
- choose the named form when the document is primarily declarative, programmatic, or documentary
